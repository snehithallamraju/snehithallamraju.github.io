About Me

I am a data and analytics leader with over 16 years of experience in the business intelligence and analytics domain, currently working as the Director of Data at RSM US India office. I have successfully completed an Advanced Management Program in Data Science and Business Analytics from the Indian School of Business, and I hold Microsoft, SAP and Tableau certifications.My mission is to leverage data and technology to drive business value and digital transformation for companies. I have designed and implemented key data platforms, including advanced analytics and IoT platforms and frameworks, cloud migration, a data governance strategy, and the evaluation of data management technologies. I have also developed data science and modeling capabilities to enable advanced analytics use cases for RFM, regression, and segmentation. Additionally, I have expanded IoT capabilities and identified use cases across multiple business units. I am passionate about aligning data and analytics for organizations, and empowering teams to deliver innovative solutions that positively impact an organization's top line revenue and bottom line. I am motivated by building and mentoring.
